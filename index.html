Welcome

I am a socio-legal researcher (PhD. Sociology, 2015, University of Helsinki) specialized in human rights protection in Russia, regional human rights courts, and justice after violent conflict.

I am working on two projects:

  • A book manuscript on how Russian human rights lawyers have helped victims of grave abuses from the conflicts in Chechnya, Georgia, and Ukraine in their legal complaints against Russia.
  • Several articles that examine how Russian human rights lawyers invent new ways to protect the freedom of information in an environment that has become increasingly hostile to human rights.

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Erik Castrén Institute for International Law and Human Rights, University of Helsinki, Finland, and a honorary research associate at the Liu Institute for Global Issues, University of British Columbia, Canada. Previously, I worked for the Aleksanteri Institute for Russian and East European Studies at the University of Helsinki, Finland. I am part of Russia Media Lab, an interdiciplinary network focussing on the freedom of expression and media in Russia.

Special Issue "Critical Perspectives on the Security and Protection of Human Rights Defenders" The International Journal of Human Rights 19 (7): 979-998

Our special issue was endorsed by the United Nations Special Rapporteur on the Situation of Human Rights Defenders:

"In the face of growing attacks on human rights in many countries, the risks faced by people who defend their universality have intensified. Human rights defenders increasingly face criminalization, persecution, arbitrary arrest and detention. They are threatened, physically attacked and assassinated. As we mark the 17th anniversary of the UN Declaration on Human Rights Defenders we should ask ourselves what more must be done to better protect defenders and promote their work. I welcome this Special Issue of the International Journal of Human Rights. It compels us to confront these challenges and to discuss critical questions, both among scholars and practitioners. Our next common goal is to translate this research into practical tools to strengthen the protection regime for human rights defenders", Michel Forst, UN Special Rapporteur on the Situation of Human Rights Defenders
